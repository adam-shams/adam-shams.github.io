Career Goals

Where I'm headed, and the steps I'm taking to get there.

Long-Term Vision

I want to work full-time in industry building impactful AI and machine learning systems. My goal is to be at the forefront of applied AI — developing technology that solves meaningful problems in healthcare, safety, education, and beyond. I'm drawn to roles that combine deep technical work with real-world deployment, where the systems I build make a tangible difference in people's lives.

Short-Term Goals (2025–2027)

  • Land a Summer 2026 internship in software engineering, machine learning, or AI research at a company pushing the boundaries of applied AI.
  • Continue and expand undergraduate research at the GT/Emory Bio-MIBLab and pursue new lab opportunities in NLP and AI systems.
  • Deepen involvement in AI safety through AISI (AI Safety Initiative at Georgia Tech), contributing to responsible AI development practices.
  • Complete foundational coursework in Data Structures & Algorithms and Digital System Design, building toward upper-level ECE and CS electives in AI/ML.

Roadmap: 5 Concrete Steps

Spring 2026 — Ongoing

Build Research Experience

Continue work at the Bio-MIBLab, expanding into new research directions. Seek additional lab opportunities in NLP, multimodal AI, or AI for healthcare. Target co-authoring publications and presenting at conferences (building on ENDO 2025).

Spring 2026 — Ongoing

Contribute to Open-Source AI

Continue contributing to the Langroid framework and explore other open-source AI projects. Build a public track record of high-quality contributions that demonstrate both technical skill and collaborative ability.

Fall 2025 — Spring 2027

Complete Core GT Coursework

Finish Data Structures & Algorithms (CS 1332) and Digital System Design (ECE 2020), then advance to upper-level courses in machine learning, computer architecture, and AI systems. Use coursework to deepen theoretical foundations.

Summer 2026

Secure an Industry Internship

Apply to AI-focused companies, research labs, and tech firms for a Summer 2026 SWE or ML internship. Leverage research experience, open-source contributions, and project portfolio to stand out. Target companies where I can work on applied AI/ML systems.

Ongoing

Engage with AI Safety & Ethics

Stay active in AISI and the broader AI safety community. Attend workshops, participate in discussions, and ensure that safety and ethics considerations inform every system I build. Responsible AI development is not optional — it's foundational.
